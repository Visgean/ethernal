Ethernal

Ethernal is Ethereum blockchain explorer created by Visgean Skeloru. The aim of this project is to display the blockchain in its true form and as it is. Remember that the head of the chain can change as newer block distribute across the network. Thus information presented should always be verified on your own node and should be used with caution.

All prices, costs and taxes are displayed in Ether. At the moment you can't see transactions for account, this is important issue to solve and I will try to solve it as soon as possible. You can click at most of the transactions, block and accounts hashes to get to detail.

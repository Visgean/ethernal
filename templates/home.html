Ethernal - blockchain explorer

Ethernal is Ethereum blockchain explorer created by Visgean Skeloru. The purpose of creating another blockchain explorer was to educate myself with Ethereum data structures and to explore the data behind the network.

Down there you can see latest Ethereum block as it was returned from Geth node. Some of the fields are modified - bloom filter is shortened. All prices, costs and taxes are converted to Ether.
